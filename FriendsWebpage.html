Could Joey BE wearing anymore clothes?

Employing a teaching approach known as the Socratic Method Joey asks the viewer if he could, in fact, wear any more clothes, prompting the audience to ponder life's limitations and their place in the universe.

Most people believe Joey could in fact wear more clothes until one of three conditions are met:

  1. He runs out of clothes
  2. He is so bulky he cannot move enough to put on more clothes
  3. He has suffocated from too many clothes
